My Top 5 Favorite South Indian Movies

  1. Baahubali: The Beginning
    • Year Released: 2015
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah
    • Description: An epic historical fiction film that follows the journey of a young man who discovers his royal heritage and his role in an ancient kingdom's destiny.
  2. K.G.F: Chapter 1
    • Year Released: 2018
    • Main Actors: Yash, Srinidhi Shetty
    • Description: A period drama about the rise of a poor boy from the streets to becoming the most feared man in Kolar Gold Fields.
  3. Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo
    • Year Released: 2020
    • Main Actors: Allu Arjun, Pooja Hegde
    • Description: A light-hearted family drama that revolves around the complexities of family relationships and a young man's journey to discover his true identity.
  4. Vikram Vedha
    • Year Released: 2017
    • Main Actors: R. Madhavan, Vijay Sethupathi
    • Description: A neo-noir action thriller that explores the confrontation between a righteous police officer and a notorious gangster.
  5. Drishyam
    • Year Released: 2013
    • Main Actors: Mohanlal, Meena
    • Description: A gripping drama about a father's desperate measures to protect his family from the law after they become involved in a crime.
